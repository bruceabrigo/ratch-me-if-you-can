How to Play

BMO (the cat) is on the hunt to catch mad treatts from his owner
Help BMO get what he needs by tracking down rats and mice!
Use WASD keys to move BMO where he needs to! But...

LOOK OUT FOR TRAPS!
Traps can kill! Thus ending the game, get to 25 points and win the best treats ever.

1 Mouse = 1PT | 1 Giant Rat = 2PT
1 Trap = Game Over!!
